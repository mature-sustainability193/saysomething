Model

New here? small.en (Recommended) is the best balance for English on most machines. Download it, then set it active. Larger models are more accurate but slower and use more disk; the multilingual ones handle other languages.

Privacy

Audio never leaves your machine. Transcription runs on a local Whisper server bound to 127.0.0.1. The only time Say Something touches the internet is when you download a model.
